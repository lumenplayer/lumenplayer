# Lumen & Privacy

Lumen is designed to protect your information and minimize data collection.

- Lumen App is a video player that fetches and displays content through video sources you configure. The app itself does not host, store, or distribute any video content.
- Your watch history, favorites, and video source configurations are stored only on your device locally, and can be synced across your own devices via iCloud. Lumen does not upload any of this data to third-party servers.
- Lumen does not collect your personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number), nor does it require registration or login to any account.
- When you browse content using video sources, network requests are sent directly to the video source addresses you have configured. Lumen does not intercept, log, or forward any data in these requests.
- Lumen does not include any advertising SDKs, does not perform ad tracking, and does not share your usage data with any third parties.
- Cached data used by the app (such as page cache and plugin cache) is stored only on your device locally. You can clear it at any time in Settings.

## Data Storage

Lumen uses Apple's SwiftData framework to store your watch history, favorites, and video source configurations locally on your device. If you have enabled iCloud, this data is securely synced across your devices via Apple CloudKit. For information about how Apple handles iCloud data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

## Video Sources & Network Requests

Video sources you add through configuration files are entirely managed by you. Lumen only makes network requests to the corresponding addresses based on your configuration to fetch content catalogs and playback links. It does not send this information to Lumen developers or any third parties.

## Third-Party Content

All content obtained through video sources comes from third-party websites or services. Lumen is not responsible for the legality, accuracy, or availability of such third-party content. You should familiarize yourself with and comply with applicable laws and regulations in your region.

## Children's Privacy

Lumen does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since Lumen does not collect any personal information, there are no special data handling requirements for children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you through app updates. Your continued use of Lumen constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
